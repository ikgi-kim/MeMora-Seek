Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Expected July 6, 2026

MeMora-Seek (hereinafter referred to as the "App") values the privacy of our users and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act of the Republic of Korea as well as global privacy regulations such as the GDPR and CCPA. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how the App processes and uses information, and for what purposes.

1. Information Processed and Methods of Processing

The App may utilize the following information to provide its services:

(1) Photos and Media

The App reads photos and image files within the device that the user has selected or granted access to in order to provide media management features. The App does not upload or store your photos or images on the developer's external servers.

  • Purpose: Photo and image management, search, classification, map display, and AI image analysis.

(2) Photo Metadata (EXIF)

The App can read metadata (such as capture date, time, and GPS location) embedded in photo and image files. All metadata is processed strictly within the user's local device.

  • Purpose: Photo and image sorting, map display, and location-based media management.

(3) Current Location

The App may use the user's current location. The current location is processed locally on the user's device and is never transmitted to the developer's servers.

  • Purpose: Notifications for photos taken/created nearby, map functionalities, and providing location-based image services.

(4) AI Image Analysis (On-device)

The App may analyze photos and images using the Google ML Kit Image Labeling feature. AI analysis is performed 100% locally on the user's device (On-device), and the images themselves are never uploaded to any external server. The analysis results (e.g., labels for objects, scenery, etc.) are saved only in the device's internal database to provide search functionality.

  • Purpose: Automatic image classification and enhancement of the image search feature.

(5) Firebase Analytics

The App uses Google Firebase Analytics to improve service quality and conduct statistical analysis. The collected information is managed securely in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

  • Collected Items: App usage statistics, device information, operating system version, approximate country and regional location, crash occurrences, and feature usage patterns.

(6) Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The App may display advertisements via Google AdMob and uses Firebase Crashlytics to improve service stability. In this process, Google or its advertising partners may process device identifiers for ad delivery, performance measurement, and error log analysis. (Note: Personal photos and images are never collected through these services.)

When required, the App requests user consent for personalized advertising through the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP).

2. Retention and Destruction of Information

All data generated or analyzed by the App is, in principle, stored strictly within the user's local device. The developer does not store or retain your photos, images, locations, or AI analysis results on any external server. Data processed through Firebase and AdMob is managed according to Google's data retention policies.

3. Third-Party Sharing and International Data Transfers

The App does not sell or unauthorizedly provide your photos, images, or location information to third parties. However, in the process of utilizing the global services listed below, limited analytical data and advertising identifiers may be transferred and processed internationally (to Google servers located in the United States and other countries).

  • Recipient: Google LLC (Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, AdMob, UMP)
  • Purpose of Transfer: App quality and error improvement, usage statistical analysis, in-app ad delivery, and personalized ad consent management.
  • Items Transferred: Advertising ID, device information, app usage history, and approximate location data.

4. User Rights and Global Privacy Rights

Users can change app permission settings at any time or withdraw their consent for personalized ads through the Google UMP banner. Additionally, users can immediately remove all locally stored data by deleting the App from their device.

[EU GDPR & California CCPA Privacy Rights]
Users residing in the EU or certain US states have the right to request access to, correction of, or erasure of their data (the right to be forgotten), the right to restrict or object to processing, and the right to data portability under applicable laws. You also have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information for personalized advertising. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the inquiry email provided below.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 14 in South Korea, or under the age of 13 globally, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If it is discovered that a child's information has been collected inadvertently, it will be deleted immediately.
